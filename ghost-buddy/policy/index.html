Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 9, 2024

Ghost Buddy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo
  • Account credentials

1.2 Training Session Data

During your training sessions, we collect:

  • Session date and duration
  • Number of sets and shots completed
  • Selected shot patterns
  • Rest time settings
  • Performance metrics and statistics
  • Player position and movement data

1.3 Camera and Video Data

Ghost Buddy uses your device's camera to:

  • Detect the squash court boundaries
  • Track your position on the court in real-time
  • Analyze your movement patterns

Important: Camera data is processed locally on your device. We do not store, transmit, or share any video recordings or camera feed data. All computer vision processing happens on-device using CoreML and OpenCV technologies.

1.4 Device Information

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and build number
  • Device identifiers for analytics purposes

1.5 Usage Data

  • App features used
  • Session frequency and duration
  • App settings and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Ghost Buddy service
  • Track and display your training progress
  • Generate performance analytics and insights
  • Personalize your training experience
  • Process subscription payments
  • Send you notifications about your training sessions
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • App settings and preferences
  • Session history and statistics
  • Performance data (CSV and JSON files)

3.2 Cloud Storage

We use Firebase services to securely store:

  • User account information
  • Profile photos
  • Subscription status

3.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure authentication through Firebase
  • Protected API endpoints
  • Regular security audits

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and cloud storage
  • Apple App Store: For subscription management and in-app purchases

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information through the profile settings
  • Deletion: Delete your session data through the settings menu or request account deletion
  • Data Portability: Export your session data
  • Opt-out: Disable specific features like camera tracking

6. Camera and Permissions

Ghost Buddy requires camera access to function properly. You can:

  • Grant or deny camera access in iOS Settings
  • Revoke camera permission at any time

Without camera access, the app's core features will not function. All camera processing is performed locally on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Ghost Buddy is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

  • Session Data: Stored locally until you delete it manually
  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Data: Permanently removed within 30 days of deletion request

9. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: Authentication, Firestore Database, and Storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Apple StoreKit: In-app purchases and subscriptions (Privacy Policy)

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country where data protection laws may differ. By using Ghost Buddy, you consent to the transfer of your information to these locations.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you a notification (for significant changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

12. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

Note: We do not sell your personal information.

13. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
