Privacy Policy

AI Trading Advice App
Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI Trading Advice application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Trading Chart Images: Screenshots of trading charts you upload for AI analysis
  • Text Messages: Your questions, comments, and descriptions about trading positions
  • Profile Information: Profile image selection from system icons
  • App Preferences: Language settings, dark mode preferences, and other app configurations

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns, feature interactions, and session duration (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash Reports: Technical information about app crashes to improve stability (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Device Information: iOS version, device model, and app version for compatibility purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

Core App Functions:

  • Analyze your trading charts using AI technology
  • Provide personalized trading advice and recommendations
  • Maintain chat history for your reference
  • Process in-app purchase transactions

2.1 AI Analysis

Your uploaded trading chart images and text messages are sent to OpenAI's services to generate AI-powered trading analysis and advice. This processing helps provide you with relevant, personalized trading insights.

2.2 App Improvement

We use anonymized analytics data to understand how users interact with our app, identify popular features, and improve the overall user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 OpenAI

We use OpenAI's GPT-4 services to analyze your trading charts and provide AI-powered advice. Your data sent to OpenAI is subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

3.2 Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Analytics: Tracks app usage patterns and user engagement
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports to improve app stability

Firebase data collection is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

3.3 Apple App Store

In-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • Chat history and user preferences are stored locally on your device
  • No personal trading data is stored on our servers
  • You can delete local data by uninstalling the app

4.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmissions use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
  • We do not store your trading chart images after AI analysis
  • Keychain access is used for secure storage of user preferences

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Data Control

  • Delete Chat History: You can delete individual conversations or all chat history within the app
  • App Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
  • Analytics Opt-out: You can disable analytics in your device's privacy settings

5.2 Subscription Management

  • Manage your subscription through your Apple ID account settings
  • Cancel anytime through the App Store
  • Restore purchases on new devices

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

7. International Users

Our app is available worldwide. By using our app, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other countries where our service providers operate, which may have different privacy laws than your country of residence.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date below. Your continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
