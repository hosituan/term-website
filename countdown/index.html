Information We Collect

Countdown: Widget Ticker does not collect any personal information from its users. The app operates entirely on your device and does not transmit any data to external servers.

Data Storage

All countdown data, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard iOS storage mechanisms. This includes:

  • Your countdown timers and their configurations
  • Widget settings and preferences
  • App customization options (colors, icons, etc.)
  • Premium purchase status (handled by Apple's App Store)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Countdown: Widget Ticker uses Apple's App Store for in-app purchases. Any payment processing is handled entirely by Apple according to their privacy policy. We do not have access to your payment information.

Widgets and iOS Integration

The app creates widgets that display on your iOS home screen and lock screen. These widgets only display the countdown information you have configured and do not collect or transmit any additional data.

Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no data security concerns related to external data handling. All your information remains securely on your device, protected by iOS's built-in security features.

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special considerations regarding children's privacy under COPPA or similar regulations.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly of changes.
