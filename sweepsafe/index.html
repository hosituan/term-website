SweepSafe is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your personal information should remain personal. That's why we've built our app to store all your data locally on your device and never transmit it to our servers.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

SweepSafe does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following information:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • App usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information
  • Any data from your interactions within the app
  • Search queries or preferences
  • Learning progress or achievements

2. Local Data Storage

All information you provide to SweepSafe is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Profile Information: Your name, selected profile image, and preferences
  • Learning Progress: Completed guides, quiz results, and achievement levels
  • App Settings: Language preferences, notification settings, and customizations
  • Favorites: Any sweepstakes or content you bookmark or save

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to SweepSafe or any third parties.

3. No Internet Connection Required for Core Features

SweepSafe's core educational features work completely offline. Your learning progress, safety guides, and personal data remain on your device at all times. Internet connectivity is only used for:

  • Downloading updated sweepstakes listings (publicly available information)
  • Accessing external websites when you choose to visit sweepstakes sponsors
  • Loading news articles and safety alerts (public information)

4. Third-Party Services

SweepSafe may include links to external websites and services. When you click these links:

  • You are leaving the SweepSafe app
  • Those websites have their own privacy policies
  • We are not responsible for their privacy practices
  • We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before providing any information

5. Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • We cannot access your information even if we wanted to
  • Your data cannot be compromised through server breaches
  • You have complete control over your information

6. Children's Privacy

SweepSafe does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any personal information at all, our app is safe for users of all ages. However, we recommend parental supervision for users under 13.

7. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Reset Progress: Use the in-app reset feature to clear your learning progress
  • Delete App: Uninstalling the app removes all stored data from your device
  • Clear Settings: Reset app settings to default values anytime

Since no data is stored on our servers, there's nothing for us to delete on our end.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make changes:

  • The updated policy will be posted in the app and on our website
  • The "Last Updated" date will be revised
  • Significant changes will be communicated through app updates

Your continued use of SweepSafe after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Your Rights

Since we don't collect your personal information, traditional data protection rights don't apply in the usual way. However:

  • You have complete control over all data on your device
  • You can request information about our privacy practices
  • You can ask questions about how the app works
  • You can report any privacy concerns
